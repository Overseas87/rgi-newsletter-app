FEATURE ADDITION: DAILY INTELLIGENCE BRIEF (FULL-DAY SYNTHESIS)
Objective

Upgrade the system from generating single-article summaries to producing a comprehensive daily intelligence brief.

The system must:

analyze ALL relevant content from the day
identify what actually matters
synthesize it into ONE coherent, high-quality article
🧠 1. DAILY AGGREGATION LAYER (CRITICAL)

Instead of treating each article independently:

The system must:

collect all relevant items from the last 24 hours:
news articles
social media signals (Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
group them by:
topic
event
narrative
🔍 2. SIGNAL vs NOISE FILTERING (VERY IMPORTANT)

The system must NOT include everything.

It must:

filter out low-relevance content
prioritize:
high-impact developments
emerging trends
strategic signals

Goal:
👉 reduce 100+ inputs → 5–10 key themes

🧩 3. THEMATIC CLUSTERING

Group content into major themes such as:

AI & Technology
Geopolitics
Markets & Economy
Leadership & Organizations
Environmental / Societal

Each theme must:

contain multiple sources
represent a real “storyline” of the day
✍️ 4. DAILY BRIEF GENERATION (CORE OUTPUT)

Generate ONE main article per day:

👉 “RGI Daily Strategic Intelligence Brief”

Structure:
1. Headline

Clear, high-level summary of the day

2. Executive Summary (VERY IMPORTANT)
5–7 bullet points
what matters today
quick overview for busy leaders
3. Main Sections (by Theme)

For each key theme:

short explanation of what happened
synthesis across multiple sources
key developments
integration of:
news
social signals
4. Cross-Theme Insight (CRITICAL)
identify connections between topics
highlight patterns
explain broader implications
5. RGI Perspective
interpret events through:
Strategic Foresight
System Vitality
Civic Stewardship
6. Why This Matters for Leaders
practical takeaway
decision-relevant insight
📏 5. LENGTH REQUIREMENT
minimum: 800–1200 words
must feel like:
executive briefing
not a blog post
🧠 6. SOURCE INTEGRATION

The article must:

combine multiple sources
explicitly reference:
news reports
direct statements (social media)

Example:

“According to Bloomberg…”
“In a statement on X…”
⚙️ 7. USER CONTROL OPTION

Allow editor to choose:

“Auto-generate Daily Brief”
OR
“Select articles manually → generate brief”
🧠 8. PRIORITY RULE

The system must:

prioritize insight over completeness
focus on what matters, not everything
avoid repetition
⚠️ FINAL RULE

The output must answer:

👉 “What happened today — and why does it matter for leaders?”

NOT:
👉 “What articles exist today?”
