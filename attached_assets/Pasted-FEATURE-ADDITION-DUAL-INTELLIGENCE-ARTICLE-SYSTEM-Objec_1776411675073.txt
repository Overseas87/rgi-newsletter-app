FEATURE ADDITION: DUAL INTELLIGENCE ARTICLE SYSTEM
Objective

Upgrade the system to generate two distinct types of articles:

A Daily Intelligence Brief (macro overview of what matters today)
Multiple Topic-Specific Articles (deep dives into individual topics)

Both must coexist and serve different purposes.

🧠 1. DAILY INTELLIGENCE BRIEF (MACRO VIEW)

The system must generate:

👉 1 main article per day

This article must:

synthesize all major developments
connect different topics
explain what matters overall
Structure:
Headline (broad, high-level)
Executive summary (5–7 bullet points)
Key themes of the day
Cross-topic connections
RGI perspective
Leadership implications
Goal:

👉 Answer:
“What happened today — and why does it matter?”

🧩 2. TOPIC-SPECIFIC ARTICLES (MICRO VIEW)

The system must ALSO generate:

👉 multiple topic-based articles per day

Each article:

focuses on ONE topic only
uses multiple sources
provides deeper analysis
Example topics:
AI & Technology
Geopolitics
Finance & Markets
Leadership & Organizations
Climate & Environment
Structure:
Topic-specific headline
Executive summary
Key developments within that topic
Supporting signals (news + social)
RGI perspective
Leadership implications
⚖️ 3. CLEAR SEPARATION BETWEEN BOTH TYPES

The system must:

NOT mix unrelated topics into topic articles
NOT oversimplify the daily brief
Rule:
Daily Brief = broad + connected
Topic Articles = focused + deep
🧠 4. GENERATION LOGIC

Each day:

The system must:

analyze all incoming data
identify key themes
generate:
1 Daily Brief
3–6 Topic Articles (if enough data exists)
✋ 5. USER CONTROL

Allow user to:

manually trigger:
“Generate Daily Brief”
“Generate Topic Article”
select:
specific topic(s)
or let system auto-detect
📊 6. DASHBOARD STRUCTURE

Separate clearly:

Section 1:

👉 Daily Intelligence Brief

Section 2:

👉 Topic-Based Articles

Each must be visually distinct.

🎯 7. PRIORITY RULE

The system must:

prioritize high-impact developments
avoid weak or redundant articles
focus on:
business
finance
AI
geopolitics
leadership
⚠️ FINAL RULE

The system must behave like:

👉 an intelligence platform

NOT:

👉 a blog generator
