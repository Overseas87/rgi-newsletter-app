FEATURE ADDITION: PRIMARY SIGNAL DETECTION & INTEGRATION
Objective

Upgrade the system to recognize that not all valuable information comes from traditional news articles.

The system must treat:

direct statements from credible actors
official announcements
executive communication

as first-class intelligence signals, even if no news article exists yet.

🧠 1. PRIMARY vs SECONDARY INFORMATION MODEL

The system must distinguish between:

Primary Signals (HIGH VALUE)
direct statements from CEOs, companies, policymakers
official announcements
original posts on Twitter/X or LinkedIn
company blog posts or press releases
Secondary Signals
news articles reporting on those events
commentary or analysis
Rule:

Primary signals can be equal or higher priority than news articles.

⚖️ 2. AUTHORITY-BASED SCORING (CRITICAL)

Relevancy must NOT depend on format (tweet vs article).

Instead, score based on:

who is speaking
what is being announced
potential impact
Example:
Elon Musk tweet about Tesla strategy → HIGH relevance
random Twitter opinion → LOW relevance
🔍 3. HIGH-VALUE SOCIAL SIGNAL DETECTION

The system must actively detect:

major announcements
strategic decisions
product launches
policy statements
financial signals

Mark these with a label:

👉 “Primary Signal”
👉 or “Direct Signal”

🧩 4. INCLUDE PRIMARY SIGNALS IN INTELLIGENCE FEED

In the Daily Intelligence Feed:

Primary signals must:

appear alongside news articles
be clearly labeled
show:
platform (X / LinkedIn)
author name
author role (CEO, company, policymaker)
timestamp
preview
relevance score
✍️ 5. INTEGRATE INTO ARTICLE GENERATION

When generating articles or daily briefs:

The AI must:

include primary signals as core inputs
not wait for news coverage
clearly distinguish:

Example phrasing:

“In a direct statement on X, Tesla announced…”
“According to Bloomberg…”
🧠 6. PRIORITY RULE

If a high-authority primary signal exists:

it must be included in the article
even if no news article exists yet
⚠️ 7. FILTER LOW-QUALITY SIGNALS

The system must:

ignore low-authority posts
ignore opinions without information
prioritize:
decision-relevant content
original announcements
🎯 FINAL RULE

The system must understand:

👉 information is not defined by format
👉 information is defined by impact and authority
