FEATURE ADDITION: MULTI-LAYER DATA SOURCING (NEWS + SOCIAL INTELLIGENCE)
Objective

Extend the existing data sourcing system to include social media intelligence sources (Twitter/X and LinkedIn) in addition to traditional news sources.

The goal is to capture:

early signals
executive communication
real-time developments
high-value insights before they appear in news articles
🧠 1. ADD NEW DATA LAYER: SOCIAL INTELLIGENCE SOURCES

Add support for scraping and ingesting content from:

Platforms:
Twitter/X
LinkedIn
Types of sources:
official company accounts (e.g. Tesla, OpenAI, major institutions)
CEOs and executives
policymakers
academics and thought leaders
institutional accounts
🧩 2. SOURCE CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM (IMPORTANT)

Each source must be classified into categories:

Tier 1: High credibility institutional (NYT, Bloomberg, etc.)
Tier 2: Professional/analytical sources (think tanks, experts)
Tier 3: Social media sources (Twitter/X, LinkedIn)

⚠️ BUT:
Social media must NOT automatically rank low

Instead:

credibility depends on who posted
not the platform itself
⚖️ 3. CONTEXT-AWARE RELEVANCY SCORING

Update the relevancy scoring system:

Each item is scored based on:

relevance to RGI disciplines
recency (same-day priority)
source credibility
author authority (NEW)
engagement signals (optional)
Example logic:
Elon Musk announcement → high authority
random tweet → low authority
🔍 4. DISPLAY SOCIAL CONTENT IN FEED

In the “Daily Intelligence Feed”:

Include social posts alongside news articles.

Each item must show:

platform (Twitter/X or LinkedIn badge)
author name
author type (CEO, policymaker, etc.)
timestamp
short preview
relevance score
link to original post
🧠 5. SOCIAL SIGNAL DETECTION (IMPORTANT FEATURE)

Add logic to detect:

breaking announcements
major statements
emerging narratives

Mark these with a label:

👉 “Signal” or “Emerging Signal”

✍️ 6. AI SYNTHESIS MUST INCLUDE SOCIAL SIGNALS

When generating articles:

AI must:

integrate social posts into the narrative
distinguish between:
reported news
direct statements

Example:

“According to Reuters…”
“In a direct statement on X, Tesla announced…”
🧩 7. CUSTOM SOURCE INPUT (EXTENSION)

Allow editor to:

add specific:
Twitter/X handles
LinkedIn profiles
assign:
authority level
priority weight
⚠️ 8. FILTER LOW-QUALITY SOCIAL CONTENT

The system must:

ignore spam
ignore low-value opinion posts
prioritize:
original information
credible voices
decision-relevant content
🎯 FINAL RULE

The system must treat:

news → validated reporting
social → early signal / direct insight

Both are essential, but serve different roles.
